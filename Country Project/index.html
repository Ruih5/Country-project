About China

China is located in East Asia. It is the most populous country in the world. The capital of China is Beijing. China has a history of more than 5,000 years. From the invention of papermaking, gunpowder, and the compass, they are all iconic symbols of history.

History and Culture

China is a country rich in history and culture, perfectly blending ancient traditions with modern development. There are many landmarks and diverse landscapes. Visitors can experience a magnificent mix of mountains, tranquil rivers, and bustling city centers. Food, festivals, and different cultural styles are also highlights.
